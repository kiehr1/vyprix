Vyprix VPN — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [May 16, 2026]

Vyprix VPN, operated by [C.E.B.P], provides VPN, hotspot, and proxy-sharing services. This Privacy Policy explains how we access, use, and protect information when you use our application.

Information We Access

  • Network and Connection Information: Used to establish and manage VPN tunnels, hotspot sharing, and proxy connections. This information is processed locally on your device.
  • Device and Operating System Information: Used for diagnostics, performance monitoring, and compatibility support.
  • Wi-Fi and Location Permissions: Certain Android versions require location permissions for Wi-Fi device discovery. Vyprix VPN does not collect or transmit your location data for analytics or advertising purposes unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires it.
  • Certificates and VPN Profiles: VPN certificates, configuration files, and private keys are stored locally on your device to authenticate secure VPN connections. Private keys remain on-device unless you explicitly export them.

Data Storage and Sharing

  • Traffic Data: Vyprix VPN does not collect, inspect, log, or transmit your browsing traffic to our servers by default.
  • Optional Cloud Features: If cloud backup or configuration synchronization features are enabled, files are uploaded only with your explicit consent.
  • Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell personal data to third parties. Aggregated and anonymized usage statistics may be shared only if you opt in.
  • Retention: VPN profiles, certificates, and related configuration data remain on your device until you manually delete them.

Security

  • Encryption and Protection: The app uses industry-standard cryptographic libraries and secure protocols to protect VPN tunnels and credentials stored on-device.
  • Access Control: Certificates and private keys remain under your control. You may revoke permissions or remove stored profiles directly from the app at any time.
  • Secure Defaults: Backups are disabled by default, and insecure cleartext traffic is blocked whenever possible.

Your Privacy Choices

  • Permission Controls: You may revoke Wi-Fi or location permissions anytime through your device settings.
  • Profile Removal: You can delete VPN profiles, certificates, and stored credentials from within the app.
  • Diagnostics Opt-Out: If anonymized diagnostics or usage analytics are offered, you may opt out from the app settings.

Third-Party Components

Vyprix VPN may use third-party libraries and open-source software components. A list of applicable licenses and acknowledgments can be found in the app’s About or Legal section.

Children’s Privacy

Vyprix VPN is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
